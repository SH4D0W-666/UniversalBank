About Us

Universal Bank, with more than 100k employees, is a high quality, customer centric and service driven Bank. Since inception in early 2020, Universal Bank has grown into a ‘Full Service Commercial Bank’ providing a complete range of products, services and technology driven digital offerings, catering to corporate & retail customers.We are committed to serving our millions of retail, business, wealth management, payment, commercial and corporate, and investment services customers across the country and around the world as a trusted financial partner. Universal Bank operates its Investment banking & Brokerage businesses through Universal Securities and its Mutual Fund business, both wholly owned subsidiaries of the Bank. Headquartered in Mumbai, it has a pan-India presence across all 28 states and 8 Union Territories in India including (and not only limited to) a Representative Office in Dubai, Singapore and other International Countries.
